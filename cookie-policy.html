This Cookie Policy explains how this website (the “Site”) uses cookies and similar technologies. By using the Site, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit.

2. How This Site Uses Cookies

This Site uses cookies only through Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site and to improve its performance and content. No cookies on this Site are used for:

  • Advertising
  • Tracking across different websites
  • Personal identification

2.1 Google Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the Site
  • Browser type and device type
  • Approximate geographic location
  • Navigation paths and click interactions

This information is collected in an aggregated, anonymized form. I use it only to analyze traffic and improve the Site’s content and usability.

To learn more about how Google uses cookies, you may visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies .

3. Types of Cookies Used

The Site may use the following types of cookies through Google Analytics:

  • Analytics Cookies – measure how users interact with the Site
  • Performance Cookies – help identify issues such as slow-loading pages
  • Session Cookies – temporary cookies that expire after you leave the Site
  • Persistent Cookies – remain on your device for a set period to recognize repeat visits

4. Managing Cookies

You have the ability to manage or block cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to:

  • Block cookies completely
  • Delete cookies already stored on your device
  • Receive notifications when cookies are being set

4.1 Opting Out of Google Analytics

If you prefer not to allow Google Analytics to collect data, you may install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on .

5. Third-Party Cookies

This Site does not intentionally use any third-party advertising cookies or tracking cookies. However, third-party websites linked from this Site may use their own cookies. I do not control those cookies and am not responsible for their use.
